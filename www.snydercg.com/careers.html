Positions at Snyder Construction Group

We strive to produce projects that are of high quality, on budget, on schedule, and safe. We also prioritize building and fostering relationships among all project team members.

Superintendent

A qualified candidate will have experience building commercial projects in a diverse field of market sectors. They oversee construction in the field, provide leadership to field foremen, manage trade partners, and ensure successful completion of trade work. Superintendents also work closely with the Project Manager to ensure timely and quality project completion.

Contact us to Apply
Project Manager

The Project Manager is the primary leader of the project and is directly accountable for the safety, financial performance, and team development on site. They lead the project team in the construction of the entire project. This position is responsible to provide effective leadership to the project staff and ensure quality and timely completion.

Contact us to Apply
Estimator

The Estimator is the primary leader of the pre-construction process of the project. They are responsible for generating complete and comprehensive estimates, budgets, construction schedules, quantity takeoffs, scopes of work, and a thorough bid document review process.

Contact us to Apply
Assistant Project Manager

This is generally the initial step in taking an office project leadership role. They work closely with the Project Manager to ensure safe, timely, quality completion of projects. May be solely responsible for a segment of a project and/or have Engineers and Interns reporting to them.

Contact us to Apply
Assistant Superintendent

This position is generally the initial step in taking a field leadership role. The Assistant Superintendent works closely with the Project Superintendent to ensure safe, timely, quality completion of projects. May be solely responsible for a segment of a project and/or have Engineers and Interns reporting to them.
